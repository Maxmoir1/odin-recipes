Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Two different types of cheese of your choice
  • Ground beef
  • Fresh flat noodles
  • Time and patience

Steps

  1. Layer pasta flat inside a glass container
  2. Fill layered glass with 1 layer of cheese
  3. Cover layer of cheese with ground beef
  4. Cover ground beef with two layers of cheese
  5. Place in oven on 350 degrees for 1 hour
